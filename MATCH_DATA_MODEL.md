# MATCH Data Model

## Core Operating Objects

### Life Vision

Permanent guiding vision that defines the ultimate purpose and direction of Mateo's life.
### Domain

A permanent area of responsibility that requires ongoing stewardship, attention, and leadership.

Domains do not have an end date.

Objectives, projects, decisions, risks, opportunities, commitments, and metrics may belong to one or more Domains.

### Annual Objective

A major outcome to be achieved within a year.

Must support the Life Vision.

### Quarterly Objective

A major outcome to be achieved within a quarter.

Must support an Annual Objective.

### Project

A coordinated effort with a defined outcome.

Must support one or more Objectives.

### Task

A specific actionable item.

Must support a Project or Objective.

### Commitment

A promise, obligation, responsibility, or agreement that requires follow-through.

### Decision

A conclusion that determines future actions, priorities, or resource allocation.

### Risk

A factor that could negatively impact objectives, projects, commitments, or outcomes.

### Opportunity

A factor that could accelerate progress, improve outcomes, create value, or reduce risk.
